Grounded in research

Built on the science of the slow breath.

The extended exhale activates parasympathetic tone via the vagus nerve, lowering heart rate and signaling safety to the brain. Each protocol draws on peer-reviewed research in respiratory physiology and behavioral medicine.

  • Brown & Gerbarg, 2005 — sudarshan kriya & vagal tone
  • Jerath et al., 2006 — slow breathing & the autonomic nervous system
  • Ma et al., 2017 — diaphragmatic breathing on attention & cortisol
  • Gerritsen & Band, 2018 — breath-controlled vagal regulation
